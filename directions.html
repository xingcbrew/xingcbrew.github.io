Getting to Gosling's

From Toronto Coach Terminal (Megabus/ Greyhound)

It is one easy bus ride along Dundas St West. Take the 505 bus towards Dundas West Station. Hop off at Denison Avenue and walk 3 minutes (south one block on Denison Avenue, turn left/ east one block on Grange Avenue, and turn right/ south on Augusta Avenue for about 50 steps).

From Pearson Airport

Hop on the UP Express and hop off at the Bloor GO Station. Walk to Dundas West Station and take the 505 bus on Dundas Street towards Broadview Station. Hop off at Denison Avenue and walk 3 minutes (south one block on Denison Avenue, turn left/ east one block on Grange Avenue, and turn right/ south on Augusta Avenue for about 50 steps).

From Union Station

Take the subway two stops to Osgoode Station. Head to street level and take the 501 Humber streetcar 8 stops to Augusta Avenue. Walk north on Augusta Avenue for about 4 minutes. You'll find us on the left!

STREETCARS AND BUSES

Streetcars/buses run along the four major streets around us - Spadina, Dundas, Bathurst, and Queen - all just a block or two away. And each will take you to a nearby subway station, if not directly to where you want to go!
