About

Hello! I'm Xing, and I live in Toronto with my husband and our two young children. I work part time as a data scientist, graphic designer, and web developer. Learning American Sign Language (ASL) has become a hobby of mine, and I recently had the opportunity to teach basic ASL to the children in my daughter's kindergarten class. To enhance their learning experience, I wanted to create printable handouts featuring the ASL alphabet and personalized name cards for each student. Rather than designing each name individually, I developed a tool to easily generate custom ASL names. Recognizing its potential value, I decided to turn this tool into a website to benefit teachers, parents, and anyone interested in ASL. I also added Braille letters and will be adding more tools and features soon.
