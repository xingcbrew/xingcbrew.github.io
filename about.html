About

Hello! I'm Xing, and I live in Toronto with my husband and our two young children. I work part time as a data scientist, graphic designer, and web developer. Learning American Sign Language (ASL) has become a hobby of mine, and I recently had the opportunity to teach basic ASL to the children in my daughter's kindergarten class. To enhance their learning experience, I wanted to create printable handouts featuring the ASL alphabet and personalized name cards for each student. Rather than designing each name individually, I developed a tool to easily generate custom ASL names. Recognizing its potential value, I decided to turn this tool into a website to benefit teachers, parents, and anyone interested in ASL. I've chosen not to monetize this site with ads or charge for using the tool. However, if you find this resource helpful and would like to support its maintenance, I welcome any voluntary donations. Thank you for your support!
