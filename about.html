About

My name is Xing and I grew up in Toronto. I am very interested in environmental and social sustainability. I recently developed an interest in data analysis and map making and thought I'd share some of my work. Most of my data on Toronto comes from the City's Open Data Catalogue and StatsCan. I do my analysis and map making using the ggplot2 and leaflet packages in R. All my code can be found on my github page.
