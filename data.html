Refugees in Canada

Each year, Canada welcomes refugees from around the world. Recently, the Syrian crisis has resulted in several government-assisted and privately sponsored refugees being resettled in Canada. Thousands of refugees also come into Canada and file for asylum upon arrival. Here are some graphs and other visualizations of data on refugees who have come to Canada since 2011.

Homelessness and Shelters in Toronto

In 2013, there were an estimated 5,253 individuals sleeping outdoors and in emergency shelters, Violence Against Women shelters, and in health and correctional faciltiies in Toronto. According to the City, 93% of homeless people would like a permanent home. Here are some data and maps on homelessness in Toronto.
