Places to eat in Toronto

November 2019

Toronto is big on food. With over 7,000 restaurants, it's hard to not find what you're looking for. Even though we don't have much "Canadian" food to offer, we've got some of the best international cuisine from Thailand to Jamaica, and Spain to Ethiopia. Here are some of our favourites and a few guest recommendations!

Good places nearby:

  • Rasta Pasta - Jamaican/ Italian cuisine - 61 Kensington Avenue IG: @eatrastapasta
  • Seven Lives - tacos y mariscos - 69 Kensington Avenue IG: @sevenlivesto
  • Dirty Bird - chicken and waffles - 79 Kensington Avenue IG: @dirtybirdto
  • Pho Hung - Vietnames cuisine - 350 Spadina Avenue
  • Saigon Lotus - vegan/vegetarian Vietnamese cuisine - 6 St. Andrew Street IG: @saigonlotustoronto
  • Magical Taste of China - Northwestern Chinese cuisine - 405 Dundas Street West
  • House of Gourmet - Hong Kong style cuisine - 484 Dundas Street West .. with the biggest menu ever
  • Kinton Ramen - "the ultimate Japanese ramen experience" - 402 Queen Street West

Guest recommendations:

  • Pai - Thai cuisine - 18 Duncan Street IG: @paitoronto
  • Gusto 101 - fresh Italian pasta - 101 Portland Street IG: @gusto101to
  • Doomie's - vegan burgers and waffles - 1346 Queen Street West IG: @doomiestoronto
  • Egg Bae - eggy breakfasts - 189 Augusta Avenue IG: @eggbaeto
  • The Three Brewers - multi-location brewery - 120 Adelaide Street West
  • Kupfert & Kim - wheatless and meatless brunch - 140 Spadina Avenue IG: @kupfertkim

Craving something specific? Ask us about other options not on our lists. Or feel free to make a suggestion if you have another great find in mind! Let us know!
