  Opportunities

Staying in town for a few weeks and interested in getting involved at Gosling's Landing? Check out what we are looking for help with and get in touch if you think you're a good fit!


hello@goslingslanding.ca

Front desk (starting Dec/Jan)

  • Welcome guests
  • Collect payments
  • Show guests to rooms
  • Make sure everything is in place
  • Be friendly and sociable :)

Neighbourhood tour guides

  • Bring a group of guests on a tour of the neighbourhood
  • Learn fun facts about the city
  • Be a good storyteller

Food/bar crawl guides

  • Lead crawls to nearby restaurants/bars
  • Be outgoing and responsible
  • Being a foodie is a plus!
