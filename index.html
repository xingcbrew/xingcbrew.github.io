About Me

A brief introduction

Hi, I'm Xing. I grew up in the heart of Toronto, surrounded by diverse people, delicious food, and colours galore. This has contributed to a very vibrant life and my enthusiasm for trying new things. I love traveling, meeting new people, playing music (piano, Chinese harp, ukulele), playing sports (soccer, basketball, ping pong, frisbee, etc.), and cooking.

I recently completed an MSc in Sustainable Development (an Erasmus Mundus program which consisted of spending a semester each in Italy, Belgium, France, and China). I would love to ultimately work for an organization or company that shares my values for promoting social and environmental sustainability. I also love working with young people and getting them excited about important issues like sustainability, education, and inter-cultural dialogue.
