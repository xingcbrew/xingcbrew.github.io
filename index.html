Who We Are

Gosling’s Landing is a newly renovated, family run co-living space providing young travellers a home away from home. Tucked into a quiet neighbourhood in the heart of Toronto, this is a great place to meet other travellers and see all that the city has to offer. We have events such as family dinners, boardgame nights, evening socials, and organized outings to help everyone get connected.

We have a nice and big common area with comfy couches and a workspace for those looking to get in a few hours on the laptop. Our kitchen is also well equipped for travellers who like to cook. Whether you’re here for a few days or a few weeks, we’ll take you under our wing. No Gosling gets left behind!

Location

We are nested right next to Toronto’s Kensington Market/Chinatown neighbourhood, surrounded by an endless supply of local restaurants, cafes, bars, and shops. Other nearby sites include Graffiti Alley, City Hall, Yonge & Dundas Square, CN Tower, and Harbourfront. Located a block or two away from four major bus/streetcar routes heading in all directions, it's easy to get around town. As we're on a quiet street, you can sleep without the sound of traffic while also being minutes away from all the excitement!
